Why Choose us?

Code & Coffee is where the aroma of freshly brewed coffee meets the buzz of technology. Located in the heart of India, our cozy café welcomes tech enthusiasts and coffee lovers alike. Whether you're a computer science student looking to write code or just seeking a relaxing spot for a cup of joe, we've got you covered. Our menu features a variety of coffee blends from around the world, accompanied by delicious pastries and snacks to fuel your coding sessions. With free Wi-Fi and a laid-back atmosphere, Code & Coffee is the perfect place to sip, chat, and code your way to greatness
